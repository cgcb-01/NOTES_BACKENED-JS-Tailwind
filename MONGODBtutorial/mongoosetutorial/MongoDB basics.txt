And collection of documents  are called Collections.
And all collections are combined and called as Database.

------------------------------------
1. A document is a set of key-value pairs that represent a single record within a collection.

2. A field is a specific piece of data within a document that holds a value.

3. A collection is a group of related documents that share a common structure and are stored in the same database.

4. Database is a container for collection of JSOn-like documents
